Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard | Excel

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales performance using Excel Pivot Tables, KPI analysis, and dynamic dashboards.
The objective is to uncover business insights related to product categories, customer segments, regions, and yearly sales trends.

Dataset Overview

The dataset contains retail transactional data including:

Order date and shipping date

Customer details and segment

Product category and sub-category

Sales revenue and quantity

Profit and profit margin

Country, state, market, and global regions

This data supports detailed performance and profitability analysis.

Data Analysis Performed

Pivot table modeling was used to analyze:

Year-wise sales, profit, order count, and profit margin trends

Sales and performance by product category and sub-category

Segment-wise sales contribution

Regional and country-wise sales distribution

Top and Bottom performing sub-categories

Relationship between sales and profit through scatter analysis

Custom KPIs were calculated for:

Total Sales

Total Quantity Sold

Total Profit

Count of Orders

Profit Margin Percentage

Dashboard Features

An interactive Excel dashboard was built using:

Slicers for Category, Segment, State, Market, and Region

Pivot charts including line, bar, pie, donut, map, and scatter visuals

Category-wise, Segment-wise, and Region-wise KPIs on a single screen

Visual hierarchy for quick decision-making

The dashboard enables users to filter and drill down into any customer or product segment to evaluate performance.

Project Goals

Identify sales growth opportunities across different markets

Highlight top and bottom contributors to revenue

Improve product strategy through sub-category performance trends

Provide a simplified business overview with interactive controls
